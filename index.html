I am a Research Scientist at Space & Astronomy, CSIRO. My research involves using pulsar signals to search for gravitational waves and for societal applications. I also use radio telescopes to investigate dynamic behaviour of galactic radio sources, including active stars, pulsars, and unknowns.

I completed my PhD at The University of Sydney and CSIRO Space & Astronomy in 2020, on "Cool Stellar Activity at Low Radio Frequencies".

My publications are listed on SAO/NASA ADS and on my ORCiD record.

Outside of my research, I enjoy cooking, baking bread, bushwalking, and the footy.
